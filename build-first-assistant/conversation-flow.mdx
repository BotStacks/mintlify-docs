## 1. Introduction to Botstacks

### What is Botstacks?

Botstacks is a user-friendly platform designed for both beginners and skilled developers to create advanced conversational AI applications without needing coding experience. 
Whether you're interested in building chatbots or AI assistants, Botstacks provides all the tools and resources necessary to turn your ideas into reality.
