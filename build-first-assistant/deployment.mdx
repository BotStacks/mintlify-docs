Now that you have tested your assistant, it's time to deploy it to your website.

## Publishing the Bot Stack
All the changes you've made so far are stored in your account as a draft. In order to deploy your assistant, you first have to publish it to your draft.

Click the 'Publish' button at the top right of your screen to publish your draft to the live version.

<img 
height="500"  
src="/images/build-first-assistant/publish-button.png"
/>

## Deploying the Assistant

Navigate to the Web Widget menu to customize how your assistant will look when deployed on your website.

<img 
height="500"  
src="/images/build-first-assistant/web-widget-customization.png"
/>

Once you have customized the appearance of your assistant, copy the code snippet and paste it before the closing `</body>` tag on all  the pages of your website where you want the assistant to appear.

<img 
height="500"  
src="/images/build-first-assistant/web-widget-snippet.png"
/>
