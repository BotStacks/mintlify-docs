Facebook Messenger Integration makes it easy for your AI Assistant to connect with Messenger. 

Once set up, your Facebook audience can chat with your Assistant just by sending messages to your Facebook Page.

## Prerequisites

<CardGroup cols={2}>

  <Card title="A Meta Developer Account" icon="meta" iconType="solid" href="https://developers.facebook.com/">
  </Card>

</CardGroup>


To setup Messenger Integration you will need 3 pieces of information

<Steps>

  <Step title="Webhook Callback URL">
  Messenger uses the webhook callback URL to communicate to BotStacks.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Webhook Verify Token">
  BotStacks checks the webhook verify token to make sure it's the right connection with Messenger.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Messenger Access Token">
  BotStacks uses the Access token to confirm with Messenger when sending responses from the AI Assistant.
  </Step>

</Steps>


## Setting up the Messenger integration


### 1) Create an App

Visit [developers.facebook.com/apps](https://developers.facebook.com/apps/) and create your app by clicking the `Create App` button


<img 
height="500"  
src="/images/integrations/messenger/1.png"
/>


Choose `Other` as the use case for your app

<img 
height="500"  
src="/images/integrations/messenger/2.png"
/>
 

Choose `Business` as the app type

<img 
height="500"  
src="/images/integrations/messenger/3.png"
/>


Give a `Name` and `Contact Email` for your app

<img 
height="500"  
src="/images/integrations/messenger/4.png"
/>


### 2) Setup Messenger

After you have created your app, you will be taken to the Meta Developer dashboard. From the dashboard click the `setup` option on **messenger**

<img 
height="500"  
src="/images/integrations/messenger/5.png"
/>


Navigate to **Messenger API Settings** and click `configure`

<img 
height="500"  
src="/images/integrations/messenger/6.png"
/>


Once you have clicked the configure button, you will be asked to enter `Callback URL` & `Verify Token`

<img 
height="500"  
src="/images/integrations/messenger/7.png"
/>


On BotStacks dashboard, go to the integrations page and copy the `Callback URL` & `Verify Token` and enter it on Meta Dashboard

<img 
height="500"  
src="/images/integrations/messenger/8.png"
/>


Once you have entered the data on Meta Dashboard, you can see the `Continue` button.
<img 
height="500"  
src="/images/integrations/messenger/9.png"
/>


### 3) Connect your Facebook page

Connect your Facebook page, this is the facebook page your assistant will be active on

<img 
height="500"  
src="/images/integrations/messenger/10.png"
/>


### 4) Generate Access Token

Click on `Add Subscription` button to add subscriptions to the webhook

<img 
height="500"  
src="/images/integrations/messenger/11.png"
/>


Select the option `messages` and click `confirm`

<img 
height="500"  
src="/images/integrations/messenger/12.png"
/>

Click the `Generate` button to generate your Access Token

<img 
height="500"  
src="/images/integrations/messenger/13.png"
/>


Copy the `Access Token` and paste it on BotStacks integrations page

<img 
height="500"  
src="/images/integrations/messenger/14.png"
/>

<Warning> DO NOT share this Token with anyone </Warning>

<img 
height="500"  
src="/images/integrations/messenger/15.png"
/>


From now on, your AI Assistant will respond to the messages people send to your Facebook Page.
